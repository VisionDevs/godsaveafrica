About GSAW

A Christ-centered political party raising Godly leaders for South Africa.

Who We Are

God Save Africa and the World (GSAW) is a political party founded in 2025 after 31 years of democracy in South Africa — based on the great number of unresolved challenges, merciless killing of women and children, looting of state resources, and the downgrading of the economy.

As a new political party in South Africa, we have been observing the political field of the world. This party believes that the whole world was created by God for His glory. The heavens, Earth and the whole humanity were created by God. We strongly believe that the challenges and problems the world is facing — God Almighty has all the power to deal perfectly with them.

In this country God should be our teacher, guider, director and leader. We see God as the only answer to all the challenges and problems of South Africa. GSAW is based on God's love, peace, justice, truth, holiness and forgiveness of sins.

We are aware of the great challenges facing the world: violence, crime, poverty, hunger, unemployment, abuse of power and state resources, torture, murder of innocent people, climate change, and the downfall of the economy. We believe people are naturally vulnerable, weak and sinful — they need rebirth, regeneration, transformation and change through the Word of God before they can operate to prevent corruption, crime and violence.

Our Vision

To have a brand-new society — to develop a new culture in the political field of South Africa and the world of making a Jesus Christ preaching party. Quality service delivery, eradication of corruption, violence, poverty, hunger and unemployment will be perfectly addressed by people who live by Christian principles and values.

Our Mission

We believe that it is only God who can change and transform people forever to eternity. To make children of God the true salt and the light of the World by becoming Councillors, Mayors, and Parliamentarians. For these reasons Jesus Christ will be at the centre of everything we do as our Saviour and life giver.

Article 3

Truths That Guide Our Behaviour

The Trinity

GSAW strongly believes in the Trinity — God the Father, God the Son, God the Holy Spirit.

God's Leadership

We strongly believe that it is only God who can perfectly lead and guide us to all successes, achievement and development.

Freedom for All

GSAW believes in the freedom of all humanity. Membership is open to all who believe in the rule and dominion of God Almighty.

Our Conviction

Two Major Challenges

This party has discovered two major challenges facing South Africa, Africa and the World: systems and satanic forces. These two forces possess the power to destroy, confuse, mislead, cheat and poison the whole world.

The world needs space to display its creativity, innovation and invention. Leadership must allow professional people — trained people in their respective fields — to guide us in solving challenges: scientists, researchers, philosophers, scholars, psychologists, sociologists, educationists, intellectuals, investigators and spiritual leaders.

The Word of God and prayer will be central in whatever we do. All members will be bound to follow the rules, procedures and obligations. Jesus Christ will be at centre of everything we do as our Saviour and life giver.
