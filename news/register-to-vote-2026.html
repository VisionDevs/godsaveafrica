Register to Vote — 20-21 June 2026!

God Save Africa and the World (GSAW) calls on all South Africans aged 18 and above to register at their nearest voting station during the registration weekend of 20–21 June 2026. Your vote is your power. Your vote is your voice.

Why Register?

The 2026 Local Government Elections present a critical opportunity to place God-fearing, service-oriented, and accountable leaders in your ward and municipality. Without registering, you cannot exercise your constitutional right to vote.

We strongly believe that the challenges and problems that South Africa is facing — corruption, violence, poverty, unemployment, service delivery failure — God Almighty has all the power to deal perfectly with them. But we need leaders who answer to God, not to greed.

"We believe in unity and diversity of all people. We also believe in a united, non-racial, non-sexist and democratic country based on the principles of universal declaration of human rights and freedom." — GSAW Constitution, Article 2

How to Register

Visit your nearest voting station on Saturday 20 June or Sunday 21 June 2026 with one of the following:

  • Green bar-coded ID book
  • Smart ID card
  • Valid Temporary ID Certificate

You can also check your registration status online at registertovote.elections.org.za.

Vote GSAW

GSAW stands for zero tolerance for corruption, quality service delivery as priority, transparent and accountable governance, job creation, and fair distribution of land and resources — all guided by Biblical principles and God's love.

On Election Day, find GSAW on your ballot paper and mark with an X. Together, we restore Godly governance to South Africa.
