Aims & Objectives

Article 2 of the GSAW Constitution — our foundational commitments to the people of South Africa.

#AllPowerBelongsToJesus

01

Unity in Diversity

We believe in unity and diversity of all people. A united, non-racial, non-sexist and democratic country based on the principles of universal declaration of human rights and freedom.

02

Social Justice

To fight for social justice and eliminate inequalities. Fair and proper distribution of the land particularly for the poor, hungry and the disadvantaged.

03

Economic Development

To promote economic development for all people. We believe in a manufacturing, saving and investing world that empowers communities and creates sustainable employment.

04

Anti-Corruption & Anti-Crime

To fight against all forms of corruption, crime and violence. Zero tolerance for abuse of power and state resources at all levels of government.

05

God-Led World

To have a God-led world where Biblical principles inform governance, policy, and community life. In this country God should be our teacher, guider, director and leader.

06

Innovation & Creativity

We believe that people's minds should be given space for creativity, innovation and invention. Trained professionals in their respective fields must guide us in solving our challenges.

07

Total Human Liberation

To promote the freedom and liberation of a total man — political, social, spiritual, economic, financial, physical, and psychological.

08

People's Empowerment

We shall promote people's empowerment. Communities must be equipped, skilled, and resourced to take ownership of their development.

09

Manufacturing & Investment

We believe in a manufacturing, saving and investing world. South Africa must industrialise, create productive capacity, and build generational wealth.

10

Land & Resources

We believe in a fair and proper distribution of the land, particularly for the poor, hungry and the disadvantaged. Resource equity is non-negotiable.

Our Policy Commitments

Clear stances on the issues that hit every home. Not empty promises — Constitutional commitments backed by structure.

Economy

Kill red tape + cadre deployment. Let small businesses breathe. Protect property rights. Buy Local First: government tenders prioritize SA companies. 1 million SMMEs = 3 million jobs.

Education

Qualified teachers, not cadres. TVET + apprenticeships first — dignity in every trade. Safe schools with values: no drugs, no gangs. "Train up a child in the way he should go."

Healthcare

Fix existing clinics first: medicine in stock, machines working, staff present. Stop corruption in health tenders. Train community health workers. Health starts at the home.

Anti-Corruption

Prevent: God-fearing leaders only. Detect: Quarterly financial reports, NEC monitoring. Punish: No position exempts anyone from discipline. Open Books + Integrity Commission.

Safety & Security

Accountable police — no VIPs above the law. Fix poverty so crime isn't the only option. Branch CPFs with monthly community meetings. Youth diversion through skills + sports.

Transparency

Monthly expense reports in plain language. 7-Day Rule: every complaint gets a written response. Ward committees with budget oversight + recall power. Independent Integrity Commission.
