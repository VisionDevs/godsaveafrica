Who We Are

About GSAW

God Save Africa and the World (GSAW) is a political party founded in 2025 after 31 years of democracy in South Africa — based on the great number of unresolved challenges, merciless killing of women and children, looting of state resources, and the downgrading of the economy.

We see God as the only answer to all the challenges and problems of South Africa. GSAW is based on God's love, peace, justice, truth, holiness and forgiveness of sins. This party believes that the whole world was created by God for His glory.

Our Vision

To develop a new culture in the political field of South Africa — making a Jesus Christ preaching party where quality service delivery, eradication of corruption, violence, poverty and unemployment will be addressed by people who live by Christian principles.

Our Mission

To make children of God the true salt and light of the World by becoming Councillors, Mayors, and Parliamentarians. Jesus Christ will be at the centre of everything that we do.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Prayer & Faith

The Word of God and prayer are central in whatever we do. Jesus Christ at the centre of everything.

Integrity

Transparent leadership, accountability, and zero tolerance for corruption at every level.

Peace & Love

GSAW is based on God's love, peace, justice, truth, holiness and forgiveness of sins.

Justice

Social justice, elimination of inequalities, and fair distribution of resources for all.

Innovation

Space for creativity, innovation and invention. Professional people must guide us in solving challenges.

Unity

United, non-racial, non-sexist and democratic. One nation under God regardless of colour, race or creed.
