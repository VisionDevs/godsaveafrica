Who We Are

About GSAW

We are God Save Africa (GSA), a newly established political party in South Africa founded on Christian principles and values.

This is a political party that has been founded by Christians that is built on Christian principles and values with an aim to restore God's will while providing quality service delivery to the people of South Africa.

We believe that the Word of God should be at the center of everything we do — guiding our policies, our leadership, and our service to the nation.

Faith-Based Leadership

Our Vision

To have a brand new society, to develop a new culture in the political field of South Africa and the world of making a Jesus Christ preaching party. Quality service delivery, eradication of corruption, violence, poverty, hunger and unemployment will be perfectly addressed by the people who live by Christian principles and values.

Our Mission

We believe that it is only God who can change and transform people forever to eternity. To make children of God to be the true salt and the light of the World by becoming Councillors, Mayors, and Parliamentarians.

Our Commitments

Policy Priorities

Our manifesto is built on 6 pillars that address the core challenges facing South Africa today.

01

Quality Service Delivery

Every South African deserves clean water, reliable electricity, proper sanitation, and maintained roads. We will ensure municipalities deliver basic services efficiently and without corruption.

02

Eradication of Corruption

Zero tolerance for corruption at all levels. We will implement transparent procurement, independent oversight, and swift prosecution of those who steal from the people.

03

Job Creation & Economic Growth

We believe in a manufacturing, saving and investing country. We will create an environment for SMMEs to thrive, promote local industry, and invest in skills development.

04

Land Justice & Housing

We believe in a fair and just distribution of the land particularly for the poor, hungry and the disadvantaged. Affordable housing and dignified living conditions for all.

05

Women Empowerment & Safety

We shall promote women empowerment across all sectors. Combating gender-based violence and ensuring equal opportunities for women in leadership and business.

06

Total Human Liberation

To promote the freedom and liberation of a total man — political, social, spiritual, economical, financial, physical and psychological. The African mind must be given space for creativity, innovation and invention.
